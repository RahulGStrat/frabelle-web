Ingredients

  • 1kg Ground Beef
  • 1tbsp Salt
  • 1tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • ¾ cup Good Life Bread Crumbs
  • 1pc Egg
  • 6-8pcs Arla Natural Gouda Slices
  • ½ cup Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Pickle Relish
  • 1 tbsp Yellow Mustard
  • 1 pack Skinny Fries Extra Crispy
  • 6-8 pcs Burger Buns
  • Lettuce, to serve
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Onion, sliced

Step-by-Step Directions

  1. Mix ground beef, worcestershire sauce, egg, breadcrumbs, salt and pepper. Chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Portion into desired weight and size. Shape into patties.
  3. Pour oil in a grill pan over medium heat. Cook patties for approximately 3 minutes on both sides. Put the sliced cheese on top and let it melt. Remove burgers from the pan and let it rest.
  4. To make the dressing, mix kewpie mayonnaise, pickle relish and yellow mustard.
  5. To assemble, spread a spoonful of the dressing on a toasted burger bun then add lettuce. Add 2 cooked patties then sliced tomatoes and onions. Top with another toasted burger bun.
  6. Cook skinny fries in hot oil until crispy. Remove skinny fries from the pan or pot and drain excess oil.
  7. Serve Daddy’s Double Cheeseburger with Skinny Fries.
